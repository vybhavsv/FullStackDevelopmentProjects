# Privacy Policy for KA-Vehicle-PUC-Check

Effective date: March 12, 2026

KA-Vehicle-PUC-Check helps users check publicly available Karnataka vehicle PUC lookup records.

## Data We Process

The app accepts a vehicle registration number entered by the user and sends that registration number to the Karnataka Transport Department website to perform the lookup requested by the user.

## Data We Do Not Sell

We do not sell personal data.

## Data Storage

The app does not require account creation. The Android app keeps a short-lived on-device cache of recent lookup results only to improve reliability and speed for repeated searches. This cache is not used for advertising.

## Third-Party Service

The app depends on the Karnataka Transport Department website for lookup results:
https://etc.karnataka.gov.in/ReportingUser/Scgr1.aspx

Results shown in the app are sourced from that website.

## Permissions

The Android app uses internet access so it can perform the requested lookup.

## Children

The app is not directed to children.

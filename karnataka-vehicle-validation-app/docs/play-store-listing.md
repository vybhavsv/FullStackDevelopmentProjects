# Play Store Listing Copy

## App Name
KA-Vehicle-PUC-Check

## Short Description
Check Karnataka vehicle PUC records with automatic petrol and diesel fallback.

## Full Description
KA-Vehicle-PUC-Check helps you look up Karnataka vehicle PUC records quickly from your phone.

Enter a vehicle registration number and the app automatically:
- checks Petrol first
- falls back to Diesel if needed
- shows the returned result directly in the app

What the app offers:
- simple registration number search
- automatic petrol and diesel fallback
- cleaner mobile-friendly results layout
- quick repeat lookups with short-lived caching for better reliability

Important note:
This app depends on the Karnataka Transport Department source website for live lookup results. If the source website is slow or temporarily unavailable, the app may also be delayed.

KA-Vehicle-PUC-Check is designed for quick lookup convenience and clear result presentation.
